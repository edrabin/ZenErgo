About

Ergonomic Risk Mitigation for NYC Law Firms, Financial, and Insurance Services

After three decades diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal conditions, and more than 20 years as an independent medical file reviewer for workers' compensation cases, Dr. Rabin recognized that many of the same problems kept arising — and most traced directly back to how people sat, typed, and worked each day.

ZenErgo Solutions brings that clinical lens directly to your office, helping individuals and organizations build healthier, more sustainable work environments before problems become injuries.
